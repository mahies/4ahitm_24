My Journey

A journey from the Philippines to moving to Vienna, with unforgettable travels and dreams for more adventures!

Born in the Philippines

I was born in the Philippines, a beautiful archipelago known for its vibrant culture, stunning beaches, and warm, welcoming people. It will always be home to me.

Moving to Vienna

After a few years in the Philippines, I moved to Vienna, Austria. The city’s rich history, stunning architecture, and incredible culture have become a significant part of my journey. I now live and study here, learning and growing every day.

Study and Exploration

While studying in Vienna, I had the chance to explore several amazing cities in Europe, each offering its own unique charm.

Vacation Destinations

During my vacations, I’ve visited:

  • Croatia - Exploring its beautiful coastline and ancient cities.
  • Valencia - Enjoying its unique blend of modern architecture and rich history.
  • Bratislava - The charming capital of Slovakia, full of castles and culture.
  • Budapest - The stunning Hungarian capital, known for its dramatic views and thermal baths.
  • Prague - Walking through the beautiful, historical streets of the Czech capital.

Future Travel Dreams

There are still many places I dream of visiting. Some of the top destinations on my list include:

  • Maldives - To relax on its white sandy beaches and explore its crystal-clear waters.
  • Barcelona - To admire the beautiful architecture and vibrant streets.
  • Bali - For its stunning landscapes, temples, and unique culture.
  • Paris - The romantic city filled with art, culture, and history.
  • Singapore - A modern metropolis blending nature, technology, and culture.
  • Dubai - For its luxurious lifestyle, towering skyscrapers, and desert adventures.
