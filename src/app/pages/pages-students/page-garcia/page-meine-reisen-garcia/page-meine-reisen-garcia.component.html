My Journey

Born in the Philippines

I was born in the Philippines. I grew up with my Grandparents while my mom and dad work overseas. At the age of 8 my mom flew to Taipeh,Taiwan. I was devastated. I cried every night. And my dad.. I never really have had a close relationship with him. Until now i am not close.

Moving to Vienna

At the age of 11 turning 12 I flew to Vienna. I didn't know the language first and I missed my homeland. Of course I still miss my country however after a long time I manage to adapt new living in this country.

Study
