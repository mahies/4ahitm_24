MY FAV SINGER - SZA

Biography

Real Name: Solána Imani Rowe
Stage Name: SZA
Date of Birth: November 8, 1989
Genre: R&B, Neo-soul, Hip Hop
Ethnicity: African American, with mixed heritage (including Native American)
Origin: St. Louis, Missouri, USA
Occupation: Singer, Songwriter
Years Active: 2010–present

Why I Like her

- SZA released her debut studio album *Ctrl* in 2017, which received critical acclaim and commercial success.
- Her music combines elements of R&B, soul, and hip hop, often exploring themes of self-worth, vulnerability, and love.
- SZA was nominated for multiple Grammy Awards and has won several Billboard Music Awards.
- Known for her unique vocal style and emotional lyricism, SZA has become one of the most influential modern R&B artists.
